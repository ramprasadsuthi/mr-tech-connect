Business Analyst – 6 Weeks (Intensive Program)

A practical, hands-on program designed to develop core Business Analyst skills including requirement gathering, Agile methodologies, process modeling, and data analysis used in modern organizations.

Ideal for: Students, IT professionals, career switchers, project coordinators, and anyone interested in business analysis, product management, or consulting roles.

📊 Week 1 – Introduction to Business Analysis

  • Role of a Business Analyst in Organizations
  • Overview of IT Projects and SDLC
  • Business Analyst vs Product Manager vs Project Manager
  • Understanding Business Problems & Solutions
  • Introduction to Agile and Waterfall Methodologies

📝 Week 2 – Requirement Gathering Techniques

  • Functional vs Non-Functional Requirements
  • Requirement Elicitation Methods
  • Stakeholder Interviews & Workshops
  • Document Analysis
  • Writing Business Requirement Documents (BRD)

🔄 Week 3 – Business Process Modeling

  • Understanding Business Processes
  • As-Is vs To-Be Process Analysis
  • Process Mapping Techniques
  • Introduction to BPMN & Flowcharts
  • Using Tools like Lucidchart / Draw.io

⚡ Week 4 – Agile Business Analysis

  • Agile & Scrum Framework
  • User Stories & Acceptance Criteria
  • Product Backlog Management
  • Sprint Planning & Agile Ceremonies
  • Using Jira for Project Tracking

🗄️ Week 5 – Data Analysis for Business Analysts

  • Excel for Business Analysis
  • Introduction to SQL for Data Queries
  • Basic Data Visualization
  • Understanding Business Metrics
  • Introduction to Power BI Reporting

🚀 Week 6 – Project Simulation & BA Documentation

  • Real Business Case Study Analysis
  • Creating BRD and User Stories
  • Stakeholder Communication Simulation
  • UAT (User Acceptance Testing) Basics
  • Final Business Analyst Project Presentation
💡 This 6-week program focuses on practical Business Analyst skills, requirement documentation, process analysis, and real-world project simulations, helping learners understand how business and technology teams collaborate to deliver successful projects.
